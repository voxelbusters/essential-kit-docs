# Notification Services Tutorial - Introduction

Welcome to the Notification Services tutorial! This tutorial is part of the Essential Kit tutorial series from Voxel Busters.

## What You'll Learn

In this comprehensive tutorial series, you will master:

- **Permission Management** - How to request and check notification permissions across iOS and Android
- **Local Notifications** - Creating and scheduling notifications that trigger locally on the device
- **Remote Notifications** - Setting up and handling push notifications from your server
- **Notification Management** - Managing scheduled, delivered, and notification settings
- **Advanced Features** - Notification triggers, custom properties, channels, and error handling

## Why Notification Services Matter for Unity Mobile Game Developers

Notifications are essential for player engagement and retention in Unity mobile games. They help you:

**Re-engage Players**: Remind players to return to your game with daily rewards, energy refills, or special events.

**Improve Retention**: Send timely notifications about limited-time offers, tournaments, or achievements to keep players coming back.

**Enhance User Experience**: Notify players about completed tasks, guild messages, or friend activities even when the app isn't running.
